Initial Consultation

We begin our relationship with you by offering a Free Initial Consultation, where our experienced experts assess the unique scope and needs of your hail damage situation and address any questions.

Insurance Processing

We simplify the complex and time-sensitive process of filing insurance claims by assisting you with document collection, form completion, and direct liaison with your insurance company, aiming for prompt and accurate claim approval to secure the best possible coverage for your repairs..

Repair & Coverage

Once your insurance claim has been approved, we proceed to the repair phase. The silver lining in dealing with hail damage is that insurance policies usually cover most or all of the repair costs. At Total Hail Restoration, we use only high-quality materials and employ experienced craftsmen to bring your property back to its original state, all while being covered by your insurance.
